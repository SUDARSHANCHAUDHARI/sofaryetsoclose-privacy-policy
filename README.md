## 🎯 About SoFarYetSoClose

**SoFarYetSoClose** is a couples-only emotional app that lets partners send gentle nudges, emojis, and short voice notes to say "I miss you" without chatting.

**Key Features:**

* 💕 **Gentle Nudges**: Send simple "I miss you" messages to your partner
* 😊 **Emoji Messages**: Share feelings with predefined emojis
* 🎤 **Voice Notes**: Record and send short 10-second voice messages
* 😴 **Sleep-Aware Delivery**: Respects your partner's sleep schedule
* 🔔 **Smart Notifications**: Gentle push notifications for received nudges
* 📊 **Nudge History**: View your sent and received nudges
* ⚙️ **Customizable Settings**: Sleep schedule, notification sounds, and more
* 🔐 **Secure Pairing**: Private invite code system for pairing with your partner
* 📱 **Offline Support**: Queue nudges when offline, sync when back online

**Learn more**: [Main SoFarYetSoClose Repository](https://github.com/SUDARSHANCHAUDHARI/SoFarYetSoClose)

## 🔒 Privacy & Security Design

SoFarYetSoClose is built with **privacy and security as core principles**. We use Firebase's secure infrastructure to protect your personal data while enabling you to connect with your partner.

### Core Privacy Principles

1. **Secure Cloud Storage**: All data is stored securely using Firebase (Google's enterprise-grade security)
2. **Encrypted Communication**: All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
3. **Partner-Only Sharing**: Your data is only shared with your paired partner
4. **Minimal Data Collection**: We only collect what's necessary for app functionality
5. **User Control**: You can delete your account and all data at any time
6. **Transparent Practices**: Clear communication about what data we collect and why

## 📊 What Data We Collect

### Account Information (Required)

* **Email Address**: Used for Firebase Authentication
  * Stored securely by Firebase Authentication
  * Used only for account creation and login
  * Not shared with third parties
* **User ID**: Generated by Firebase Authentication
  * Unique identifier for your account
  * Used to link your data across services

### User Profile Data (Stored in Firebase Firestore)

* **Partner Information**:
  * Partner ID (if paired)
  * Pairing timestamp
  * Invite code (for pairing)
* **Sleep Schedule**:
  * Sleep start time (HH:mm format)
  * Sleep end time (HH:mm format)
  * Stored so your partner can respect your sleep window
* **FCM Token**: For push notifications
  * Updated automatically by Firebase
  * Used only to send notifications to your device

### Nudge Data (Stored in Firebase Firestore)

* **Nudge Content**:
  * Nudge type (NUDGE, EMOJI, or VOICE)
  * Phrase or emoji (if applicable)
  * Timestamp
  * Sender and recipient user IDs
* **Voice Notes** (Stored in Firebase Storage):
  * Audio files (AAC format, ~160 KB max)
  * Stored securely in Firebase Storage
  * Only accessible by you and your partner
  * Can be auto-deleted based on your preferences

### App Preferences (Stored Locally)

* **Notification Sound Preferences**: Stored locally on your device
* **Voice Note Auto-Delete Settings**: Stored locally on your device
* **Onboarding Status**: Stored locally (shown once)

### Offline Queue Data (Stored Locally)

* **Pending Nudges**: When offline, nudges are queued locally
  * Stored in Room database on your device
  * Automatically synced when you're back online
  * Deleted after successful sync

## 📱 Data Storage & Retention

### Where Data is Stored

* **Firebase Firestore**: User profiles, nudges, pairing information
* **Firebase Storage**: Voice note audio files
* **Local Device**: App preferences, offline queue (Room database)

### Data Retention

* **User Account**: Retained until you delete your account
* **Nudges**: Retained until you delete your account
* **Voice Notes**: 
  * Stored until you delete your account
  * Can be auto-deleted based on your preferences (Never, After 7/30 days, After partner listens)
* **Offline Queue**: Deleted after successful sync
* **Account Deletion**: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

### Data Deletion

When you delete your account:
1. All your user data is deleted from Firestore
2. All your nudges are deleted from Firestore
3. All your voice notes are deleted from Firebase Storage
4. Your partner is automatically unpaired
5. Your FCM token is removed
6. Local app data is cleared

## 🔒 Security Measures

### Data Protection

* ✅ **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
* ✅ **Authentication**: Secure Firebase Authentication with email/password
* ✅ **Access Control**: Firestore security rules ensure only you and your partner can access your data
* ✅ **Secure Storage**: Voice notes stored securely in Firebase Storage
* ✅ **Regular Updates**: Security patches and updates applied regularly

### Security Rules

* Users can only read/write their own user document
* Users can only read nudges sent to them or from them
* Users can only upload voice notes to their own folder
* Partner access is limited to paired users only
